02  What's being broken (30-second version)

  • Guaranteed seat denied. Bylaws Art. IV §4 cumulative voting gives Unit 4 enough votes (4 > 3.2) to elect one director — yet we're kept off the board.
  • Board too small. Operating with 3 directors when Art. IV §1 (2021 amend.) requires 4.

  • Permanent freeze-out. The 3-unit bloc holds quorum + majority and excludes the 28.6% owner from all governance and financial oversight.
  • Removal-proof once seated. Art. IV §7 protects a cumulatively elected director from being voted off.
  • Statutory hooks. Fiduciary duty under N.C. Gen. Stat. §47C-3-103 & §55A-8-30; fees under §47C-4-117.
